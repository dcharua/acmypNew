Elías Charua García is a founding partner of Arias, Charua, Macías & Prum, S.C., specializing in Criminal Law, Agreements, Health Law, Advertising, Administrative and is the co-head of the areas of Intellectual Property, Entertainment Law, Pharmaceutical and Dispute Resolution. Mr. Charua has actively represented companies from Mexico, the United States, Latin America and Europe.

EDUCATION

Mr. Charua received his law degree in 2014 from Universidad Iberoamericana. In 2018 Mr. Charua will complete his master degree in “Adversarial System” at Centro de Estudios Jurídicos Carbonell.

REPRESENTATIVE MATTERS

Mr. Charua has also assisted our clients by providing legal advice and litigation strategies regarding the protection of Intellectual Property Rights before the General Attorney Office regarding the infringement of the Industrial Property Law and the Mexican Copyright Law.

Mr. Charua represents major enterprises before Mexican government authorities for the protection of rights in matters of Intellectual Property Law, Licenses and Franchises, Entertainment Law, Contract Law and Administrative Counseling and Litigation, including Amparo claims before federal authorities.

Mr. Charua represents different international and domestic corporations in obtaining registrations, licenses and permits from the Secretaría de Salud (Ministry of Health) and the Comisión Federal para la Protección contra Riesgos Sanitarios (Federal Commission for Protection from Health Risks) and prosecution of several administrative proceedings against authorities of the Secretaría de Salud (Ministry of Health), the Comisión Federal para la Protección contra Riesgos Sanitarios (Federal Commission for Protection from Health Risks).

As well, companies concerning registration and protection of all sorts of intellectual property rights and technical assistance agreements, and licensing and franchising arrangements, including administrative and judicial proceedings concerning infringement of trademarks, copyrights, patents and industrial secrets.

Mr. Charua has advised different companies regarding all types of industries, to protect and defend their intellectual property rights in Mexico and the United States, Latin America and Europe, also supporting them with the transfer of intellectual property regarding corporate transactions including trademarks, copyrights and appellations of origin, as occurred during the acquisition of different transactions.

PUBLICATIONS AND SEMINARS

Mr. Charua has participated in constitutional articles issued by the Universidad Iberoamericana, specifically “La Objeción de Conciencia: El Contrapeso de la Legalidad”, “IUS IBERO”, 2013.
