DATA PRIVACY AND PERSONAL DATA

Data Privacy and Personal Data areas are integrated by specialized lawyers with the aim of providing clients with a complete, informed and high-quality service. The development of our practice is integrated into the “boutique” concept in order to provide services that satisfy professional, personal and avant-garde needs to our clients.

Data Privacy and Personal Data areas offers its clients consulting and advisory services, such as adaptation and compliance projects regarding personal data protection (compliance), transfer agreements and national and international data processing orders, development of mechanisms and measures of self-regulation (codes of conduct), design and implementation of the response system to the exercise of rights by the holders, implementation of personal data protection policies, among others.

Arias, Charua, Macías & Prum offers its clients the performance of a Due Diligence within the framework of the relevant regultation regarding Personal Data Protection and analyze the companies in order to comply with the requirements provided by the Federal Data Protection Law, its Regulations, Privacy Notice Guideliness, the Recommendations on Personal Data Security and other legislation related to data protection.

It is important to mention that the relevant authority in charge of overseeing the proper application of the Federal Data Protection Law may require, investigate and verify compliance with the provisions provided by law. The counseling service and representation before the relevant authority aims to provide legal certainty to those responsible for personal data within the processes initiated by that authority, ensuring that at all times the process have the best advice to respond to the notified requirements, provide the pertinent information and eradicate or minimize the effect that these processes may entail.
