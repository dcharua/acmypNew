LITIGATION AND COUNSELING

Arias, Charua, Macías & Prum experience on litigation and counseling area is highlighted in the following areas:

·Administrative. Our group of professionals dedicated to this area of practice has developed and implemented comprehensive strategies for the representation and defense of the rights and interests of our clients in all legal disputes before any judicial authority in federal, state and municipal levels. Public Procurement. Our litigation area has defended the interests of its clients in the pharmaceutical and energy sectors before resolutions issued in proceedings of government procurement, either before the administrative authorities or the courts.

·Civil, Familiar and Commercial Law. The area of litigation has also been involved at the start of civil and commercial trials, including bankruptcies, in which the interests of our clients have been defended in order to demand proper fulfillment of contractual obligations and the eventual compensations for damages and loss profits caused by these breaches. This professional area also provides counseling on this subject in all areas related to Civil, Familiar and Commercial Law.

·Labor and Criminal Law. Our litigation area provides counseling and litigation. Our group has developed and implemented strategies for the representantion and defense of rights and interests of clients before any judicial authority.

·Constitutional. We have extensive and solid experience in constitutional matters, particularly in writs of amparo, arising under and in complement to all actions taken in defense of our clients, in all areas of practice of our Firm; working in close coordination with each of the respective areas of practice.

·Foreign Trade and Customs The Firm has advised and successfully represented companies in the pharmaceutical sector and medical devices, among others, in disputes related to foreign trade related to the import of health inputs, raw materials, medicines and medical devices.

·Pharmaceutical. Our group of professionals has represented and defended in full and under the highest ethical and professional standards to various clients in the pharmaceutical industry before multiple administrative and judicial authorities, initiating the legal necessary actions to demand from the sanitary authorities the monitoring and verification of products as to ensure the compliance with quality, safety and effectiveness requirements.

·Intellectual Property and Entertainment. Our group of professionals has represented and entirely defended and under the highest ethical and professional standards, clients from various industries before the corresponding agencies, including the Federal Court of Administrative Justice and to the various agencies of the Federal Court System including District Courts and Collegiate Circuit Courts, and before Mexico ́s Supreme Court of Justice, demanding respect for their exclusive rights, appealing any act or administrative ruling that goes against them or involving unfair competition. As well, as to protect trademark rights, actions have been initiated, even criminal, against counterfeit products holding our clients’ brands and to prevent the import of said products.

·Data Privacy. Our group of professionals has represented clients from varios industries before the relevant authority, as well as, counseling for preparing privacy policies, privacy notices and provide mechanisms to secure the personal data.

·Telecommunications. Our group has provided to our clients counseling on this matter and this Firm has represented clients before the Federal Institute of Telecommunications on litigation matters.
