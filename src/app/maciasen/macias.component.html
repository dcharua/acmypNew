David Macias Leon is a founding partner of Arias, Charua, Macías & Prum, S.C., specialized in Civil Law, Family Law, Commercial Law and Real Estate. Mr. Macias has actively represented companies from Mexico, the United States, Latin America and Europe.

EDUCATION

Mr. Macias received his law degree in 2015 from Universidad Iberoamericana. He is currently studying his master degree in “Civil and Commercial Law” at Universidad Anáhuac.

REPRESENTATIVE MATTERS

Mr. Macias has represented different international and domestic legal entities in the negotiation of agreements, contract breach between companies, trust funds, compliance of fiduciary duty, negotiable instruments, promissory notes and commercial litigation that involves any dispute that could be arisen. Also, has advised different companies regarding business and civil disputes, including contract claims, joint venture and corporate law, bankruptcy litigation, real estate litigation, insurance coverage, including litigation and Family Law.

He has also assisted our clients by providing legal advice in succession procedures, marital dissolution, settlement agreements / non-litigation, mediation and litigation in a public court with the recently implemented adversarial system.

Mr. Macias has participated in several civil disputes derived from leases, loans, donations, purchases, mortgages, usufruct, real estate, among others.

LANGUAGES
