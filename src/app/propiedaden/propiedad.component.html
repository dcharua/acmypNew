INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY AND ENTERTAINMENT

Our intellectual property and entertainment area is one of the main practices of the Firm. Within the development of its activities, our group of professionals have represented a variety of foreign and domestic companies engaged in different areas of industry and global scale services, in a variety of matters related to trademarks, patents, trade secrets, copyrights, domain names and other intellectual property rights.

The practice of this group also includes the development of processes of prior search and registration of trademarks and service, obtaining patents, protection of industrial secrets, registration of all kinds of copyright and related rights, including software, as well as the protection and recovery of domain names, pages and blogs in social networks.

Similarly, this group is involved in negotiations of various types of contracts, such as licensing and transmission of all kinds of intellectual property rights, franchising contracts, technology transfer and technical assistance, literary and musical editing contracts, recognition agreements paid collaboration, including incorporations of franchise and all the contracts derived from such incorporation, among others.

This group is also actively involved in identifying and negotiating royalties for public performance with different corporate governance, licensing for musical productions.

Furthermore, the group is constantly involved in administrative and judicial proceedings management and contentious remedies, including the trial of Amparo in the defense and protection of all types of intellectual property rights, including the handling of criminal action to combat product apocryphal.
